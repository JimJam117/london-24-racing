Welcome

Hello. My name is Astra London and I am driver for London Race Team, a team consisting of myself and my dad, Russ London. We have been racing for 3 years.

The first two years were spent in GT15 but we now race in GT30. We are based at Stewartby Water Sports Club.

The Boat

Our boat is a GT30 with a bright orange hull. It was painted this colour by Bexley Nunn, the previous owner and we didnâ€™t want to change it. The hull was built in 2012 and has changed hands many times over the years, belonging to Harvey Smith, Thomas Mantripp and Bexley Nunn at different points.

When we started racing in GT30 we decided to keep the hull and change the engine, meaning it has been ours for 3 years. We have named her Glow On.

Racing

Beginning in the 2015 season, my time in GT15 was rocky. As a new, inexperienced driver in a hull that was rather heavy, I struggled to keep up in races. Over the course of two years, my dad (Russ London) worked exceptionally hard to take weight off and through practise I gradually became a more skilled driver.

By the end of GT15, we had achieved a 3rd place at the 2016 Stewartby Sprint Championship. We also took part in the 2016 European championships at Stewartby where we finished second to last (which was an achievement for us) We look forward to more time in GT30.

Photography

At the 2017 international meet at Stewartby I was unable to compete however I volunteered to go on a tow boat where I met Stuart. He kindly loaned me his camera for the weekend and I had great fun passing the hours taking pictures.

Videography

I enjoy editing videos and in late 2017, I finished my documentary. This was a year-long project (including research, brainstorming ect) which I completed for my EPQ at college. The documentary details different aspects Stewartby club including history, boat classes and events. You can find it below or on my YouTube channel, where I have posted other video projects.
